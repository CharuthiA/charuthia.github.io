About

Hello! I design and validate medical devices that merge precision engineering with patient impact. My sweet spot is where biomechanics, mechanical design, and rapid prototyping intersect. I work end-to-end—concept → CAD/FEA → prototyping → verification & validation—partnering closely with clinical teams, R&D engineers, and manufacturing to bring ideas to life.

I’ve contributed to orthopedic instrumentation at Think Surgical, translational biomechanics research at the Carney Lab, and startup prototyping at RESURG. I care about rigorous testing, reproducible data, and designs that hold up under real clinical constraints. I thrive in environments like J&J, Stryker, or Boston Scientific—where precision, iteration, and reliability meet human health.

Strengths: CAD & FEA (SolidWorks, Fusion 360, ANSYS), mechanical design, tolerance analysis, and test system development. Build: Rapid prototyping (3D printing, machining), mechanical fixturing, torque/force testing, V&V execution, sensor integration. Software: MATLAB, Python (NumPy, Pandas, SciPy), Arduino/C++, data visualization, and HTML/JS for engineering tools.
