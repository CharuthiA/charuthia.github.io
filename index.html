About Me

I’m a biomedical engineer passionate about building reliable medical technologies that improve how clinicians operate and how patients heal. My work bridges mechanical design, hardware validation, and human-factors-driven iteration.

I thrive where analytical testing meets creative prototyping—transforming data into tangible design improvements.

Featured Projects

Smart Compression Therapy Sock

Embedded air-cuff system that monitors venous flow between the ankle and thigh to prevent DVT, using haptic feedback for compression control.

Orthopedic Drill Bit Optimization

Developed and validated a stainless-steel TPLO drill bit to minimize thermal osteonecrosis, measuring torque, force, and wear through coating degradation testing.

Bioimpedance-Based Tissue Health Monitor

Designed a haptic-feedback wearable to assess tissue perfusion via impedance readings, enabling early detection of pressure ulcers.

Raman Spectroscopy Scanner

Repurposed a Prusa gantry system to precisely maneuver a Raman scope for detecting cancerous spectral peaks in tissue samples.

Pressure Ulcer Prevention Wearable

Developed a soft, skin-safe sensor array and haptic alert system to redistribute load during prolonged patient immobility.

Smart Wound Healing Monitor

Integrated temperature and impedance sensors into a flexible patch for early infection detection and wound recovery tracking.
