About Me

I design and validate medical devices that merge precision engineering with patient impact. My sweet spot is where biomechanics, mechanical design, and rapid prototyping intersect. I work end-to-end—concept → CAD/FEA → prototyping → verification & validation—partnering closely with clinical teams, R&D engineers, and manufacturing to bring ideas to life.

I’ve contributed to orthopedic instrumentation at Think Surgical, translational biomechanics research at the Carney Lab, and startup prototyping at RESURG. I care about rigorous testing, reproducible data, and designs that hold up under real clinical constraints.
